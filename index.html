Diagnosed with depression in 2014.

I've been on a range of medications, I also underwent psychotherapy (twice).
I believe that the people suffering from mental illnesses have been particularly harmed by the stigmatization of those issues.

Hence, I'm resolved to be as informative on the topic as possible, hoping it will be of value to those who are suffering.

By no means am I a professional, my advice should not be treated as medical, finally, I shall confine myself to the issue of depression.
